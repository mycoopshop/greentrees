New Customers

What products and services does Green Trees Indoor Plant Hire provide?
We supply, install and maintain interior plants to a variety of spaces including offices, executive homes, holiday accommodation, cafes, clubs, RSL’s and stadiums to name a few.  We also provide short term options for open homes, airbnb accommodations, special events etc.  Take a look at our online catalogue of products to get some inspiration.

We are a small organization interested in indoor plants. Do you only look after large companies?
Not at all. We have a very varied client list that ranges from small offices to large clubs and stadiums. Our Team will discuss what is right for you and your space.

I would like to meet with one of the team to discuss interior landscaping for my organisation. Who do I speak to?
Click on the contact us form and let us know when's the best time for us to contact you or alternatively call Matilda on 0490 869 276.

What is the pricing and how can I buy?
If the design you’ve seen is on our website we can give you a price over the phone however we appreciate that no two spaces are the same so we will meet with you to discuss your exact requirements. If you’re happy to proceed we will ask you to sign an agreement and start your membership... It’s that simple.

What kind of service would you deliver?
We not only offer design solutions to enhance your environment but the service we offer is second to none. Our team will provide regular maintenance to all stock including watering, cleaning and dusting, periodic fertilizing, pruning and disease treatment to ensure that your space is always looking vibrant.
